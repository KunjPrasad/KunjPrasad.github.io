When in doubt, SOLID it out!

What does it mean?

When developing a web-application, if you ever feel confused on how to proceed, look to "SOLID" principles for guidance on how to proceed. Most of the patterns and practices for writing a great software application falls down to understanding and applying these principles. The topic is further discussed in a section within the architecture glossary.
