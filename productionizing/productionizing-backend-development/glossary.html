Architecture related terms

Architecture related terms are prposefully mentioned at the very bottom to explicitly highlight where one should think of it in hierarchy. These are important terms. However, they should not take precedence over the business itself. Comparing between have a feature out fast which is secure and works as intended but does not comply to architecture patterns, versus putting extra time to make a properly designed feature, one should choose the former, incur a technical debt and then try ti fix the debt. If your concerns is that the product managers will not allow this to happen, then that's a different discussion altogether.
